Hi, I'm Marcelo.

A computer software && infrastructure engineer. I'm passionate about programming && networking; learning new things (and sharing what I learn).

About me

Bachelor in Computer Science, with experience for over 15 years of software developer and infrastructure/networking work experience.

Researcher for AI subjects in academic projects, and programmer/developer with Neural Networks libraries, such as Fast Artificial Neural Network Library (FANN). Have experience working with IBM Watson platform for chat bots. Practical knowledge with Genetic Algorithms and others AI subjects.

Strong experience with development of network protocols: RFID anti-collision protocols during my PhD. TCP/IP stack, including SNMP network management protocol, during my master’s degree (developed a new MIB – Management Information Base to manage Linux servers).

Developed a complete NMS (Network Management System) with PHP, PostgreSQL and UCD SNMP library: graphical network maps, visual graphs of statistical analysis of network data, remote access to routers and switches, etc.

Co-managed the implementation of two NOC’s (Network Operations Center) for two internet service providers.

At IFSul (Sul-rio-grandense Federal Institute) (current work): I am federal employee working as the head of IT technical group of this public High School/College. We are responsible for all IT infrastructure of the College, such as networking and servers equipments (HP, Dell, Juniper), VoIP Cisco, servers softwares. I am a tech guy and also a lecturer, teaching some computer science classes.
